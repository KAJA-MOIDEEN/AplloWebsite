About Us

Best Medical Travel Company in Nairobi Kenya | Multispeciality Hospitals

Information Center was commissioned in 2015 with vision to serve patients within East & Sub Saharan Africa & facilitate seam less medical travel from Africa to world class Apollo Hospitals based in India.

Best Medical Travel Company in Nairobi Kenya

Apollo Multispeciality Hospitals in India offer excellent medical travel services to international patients especially for people of Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, and Africa looking for Quality yet Affordable Treatment at an economical price. Contact us now for the best International medical travel services.

Apollo information center strives to provide top-notch service in everything that we do. At the core of our job, it’s our ability to alleviate anxiety and fear in patients who have never travelled out of their home country, holding their hand at every single step and instilling in them confidence as they embark their journey to India for medical treatment.

Human touch, care & kindness at every single touch point that makes us unique & unmatchable. We have humbled through experiences of hundreds of patients travelling to India and every patient has taught us how important it is to “being human”.
